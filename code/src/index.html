Visualizing the New York Philharmonic

Since it's founding in 1842, the New York Philharmonic has spanned an illustrious career, performing over 14,000 performances and 22,000 concerts as of 2019. As the oldest symphony orchestra in the United States, it has commissioned works from leading composers of each era, and led by world renouned conductors such as Leonard Bernstein, Gustav Mahler, Antonín Dvořák, Aaron Copland, and Igor Stravinsky, to name a few.


Here, we use open source performance metadata gathered froms Github, to illuminate part of the rich history related to the New York Philharmonic.

This visualization represents the total amount of years of concerts played over years since the founding of the New York Philharmonic.
